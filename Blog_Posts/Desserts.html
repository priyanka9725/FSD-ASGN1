Chocolatey Desserts

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 4 ounces semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream, for serving (optional)
  • Chocolate shavings, for garnish (optional)

    • INSTRUCTIONS

    1. In a medium saucepan, whisk together the milk, sugar, cocoa powder, cornstarch, and salt until smooth.
    2. Place the saucepan over medium heat and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming.
    3. In a separate bowl, lightly beat the egg yolks. Gradually whisk in about 1/2 cup of the hot milk mixture into the egg yolks to temper them, then pour the egg mixture back into the saucepan, whisking constantly.
    4. Continue to cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens, about 2-3 minutes.
    5. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the chopped chocolate and vanilla extract until the chocolate is completely melted and the mixture is smooth and creamy.
    6. Strain the pudding through a fine-mesh sieve into a clean bowl to remove any lumps or cooked egg bits.
    7. Divide the pudding among individual serving dishes or ramekins. Cover each dish with plastic wrap, placing the wrap directly onto the surface of the pudding to prevent a skin from forming.
    8. Refrigerate the puddings for at least 2 hours, or until chilled and set.
    9. Before serving, top each pudding with a dollop of whipped cream and garnish with chocolate shavings if desired.
    Bon Appetit!!
