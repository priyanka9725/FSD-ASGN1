Indian Snack Bites

Spice up your snack game with Indian Snack Bites - Flavor-packed, Spicy and Juicy treats that dance on your palate!

Get ready for a flavor explosion with my Spicy Potato Cheesy Bites recipe! These delectable morsels combine the comforting goodness of potatoes with a kick of spice and oozy cheese, creating an irresistible snack sensation. Each bite is a perfect harmony of creamy, tangy cheese and zesty spices, enveloped in a crispy potato shell. Whether you're hosting a party, craving a savory snack in the midnight, or want to enjoy your me time with Binge Watching, these bites are sure to tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving more. And the best part is my recipe is more healthier than the traditional ones, without loosing it's original flavours. Serve them as appetizers, enjoy them as a midday treat, or indulge in a late-night snack – these cheesy potato bites are versatile and guaranteed to be a hit with everyone. Get ready to elevate your snacking experience with these Spicy Potato Cheesy Bites


INGREDIENTS

  • 3 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

    • INSTRUCTIONS

    1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking sheet or line it with parchment paper.
    2. Place the diced potatoes in a pot of boiling water and cook until they are fork-tender, about 8-10 minutes. Drain the potatoes and transfer them to a large mixing bowl.
    3. Mash the potatoes using a potato masher or fork until they are smooth and creamy.
    4. Add the shredded cheddar cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, bread crumbs, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper to the mashed potatoes. Mix until all the ingredients are well combined.
    5. Using your hands or a spoon, shape the potato mixture into small bite-sized balls or patties and place them onto the prepared baking sheet.
    6. Drizzle the olive oil over the potato bites and gently toss to coat them evenly.
    7. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the potato bites are golden brown and crispy on the outside.
    8. Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.
    9. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired, and serve the cheesy potato bites hot as a delicious appetizer or snack.
    Bon Appetit!!
